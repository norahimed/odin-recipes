Fudge Brownies

The best, fudgy one bowl cocoa brownies! These brownies have a super fudgy center without losing that crispy, crackly top. So simple, but SO delicious; guarenteed to be a family favorite. They are gone in seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted and hot
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 1/8 cup white granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350ยบ F
  2. Lightly grease an 8-inch square baking pan with cooking oil spray. Line with parchment paper (or baking paper); set aside.
  3. Combine hot melted butter, oil and sugar together in a medium-sized bowl. Whisk well for about a minute. Add the eggs and vanilla; beat until lighter in colour (another minute).
  4. Sift in flour, cocoa powder and salt. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until JUST combined.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan, smoothing the top out evenly.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the centre of the brownies in the pan no longer jiggles and is just set to the touch.
  7. Remove and allow to cool to room temperature.
